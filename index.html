BUMECA

Bonded Uganda Health Information Officers and Medical Coders Association

Vision Activities Services Contact

Vision

To Promote the Acquisition of Timely and Quality Health Information for Evidence Based Decision Making

Mission

To support the Medical Records Professionals to continuously commit themselves to working in a result-oriented manner

Goal

To build specialized Health Information (medical records) workforce that meets the global and national standards

What We Do

  • Patient demographic data entry
  • Data collection, aggregation, sorting and validation
  • Data analysis, presentation and reporting
  • Management of health records
  • Management of electronic health records systems
  • Community engagements on health data-related issues (research data)

Services We Offer

  • HMIS Trainings and Mentorships
  • Research Data Collection and Presentation
  • Electronic Medical Records Systems Management
  • HMIS Data Tools Printing
  • Organization Health Records Filing and Retention
  • Disease Coding and Classification Trainings
