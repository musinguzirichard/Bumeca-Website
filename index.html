About BUMECA

BUMECA is a national body uniting health records and informatics professionals in Uganda. We ensure stewardship and advancement in health information for better health outcomes.

Latest News & Updates

  • 17 July 2025: BUMECA launches data mentorship programs in Central and Western Uganda.
  • 16 July 2025: Collaboration with MoH to improve health facility records management nationwide.
  • 14 July 2025: Training on ICD coding for regional hospitals kicks off in Mbarara.

Our Mission

To support medical records professionals to commit to result-oriented service delivery through timely and quality health data collection, analysis, and dissemination.
